## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Open the Application
```bash
# Simply open index.html in any modern browser
# No installation, server, or dependencies needed!
```

### Step 2: Choose Settings
- Select difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard
- Choose mode: Timed (60s) or Full Passage

### Step 3: Start Testing
- Click "Start Typing Test" or press any key
- Type the passage that appears
- Watch your stats update in real-time

### Step 4: View Results
- See your final WPM, accuracy, and stats
- If it's a personal record, you'll get a special message! 🎉
- Try again or change settings
